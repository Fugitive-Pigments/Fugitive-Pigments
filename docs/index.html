We provide diverse opportunities, outreach, education, and support for the interdisciplinary arts, maker spaces, storytellers, and creatives of all types.

ABOUT FUGITIVE PIGMENTS

Fugitive Pigments supports the value of community creativity and the importance of amplifying marginalized and underrepresented voices in the arts. We work to shine a spotlight on extraordinary works of all imaginable—and even unimaginable—kinds.

We serve to sustain, grow, and diversify existing cultural events and groups, and springboard new ones. We support our partner groups and events via fiscal sponsorship, mentorship, and allyship, and help make them as awesome as they want to be.
